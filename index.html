About Us

Ceeyem Glass & Plywoods has been a cornerstone in providing high-quality glass and plywood solutions in Kochi, Kerala, for over a decade. We pride ourselves on our extensive range of products, commitment to customer satisfaction, and competitive pricing for both retail and wholesale clients.

Our mission is to be the leading supplier of premium glass and plywood, offering unparalleled quality and service. We understand the diverse needs of our customers, from individual home renovators to large-scale construction projects, and strive to provide tailored solutions that exceed expectations.

Located conveniently in Nettoor, Maradu, we are easily accessible and ready to assist you with your project requirements. Our experienced team is always on hand to offer expert advice and help you choose the perfect materials for your needs.
